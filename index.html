Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Pill Reminder ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

The App may collect and store:

  • Email address used for authentication.
  • User profile information.
  • Medication schedules, dosage information, and reminder settings.
  • Caregiver relationship information.
  • Push notification tokens used to deliver reminders and caregiver alerts.
  • Technical information necessary for the operation of the App.

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide medication reminders.
  • Deliver caregiver notifications when enabled.
  • Maintain your account and settings.
  • Improve the reliability and functionality of the App.
  • Provide customer support.

Data Storage

Your information is stored using Google Firebase services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Cloud Functions

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information.

Push Notifications

The App uses push notifications to deliver medication reminders and caregiver alerts. Notification tokens are stored securely and used only for delivering notifications related to the App.

Caregiver Features

If you choose to use caregiver functionality, limited information may be shared between the patient and caregiver accounts as necessary to provide notification services.

Account Deletion

Users may delete their account directly within the App. When an account is deleted, associated user data is removed according to the App's data retention processes.

Third-Party Services

The App relies on third-party services including Google Firebase and Expo Notification Services. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

Medical Disclaimer

The App is intended for informational and reminder purposes only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
