Account and Data Deletion

Pill Reminder allows users to permanently delete their account and associated data directly within the application.

No email request is required.
Users can delete their account themselves from the app at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Pill Reminder application.
  2. Navigate to the Settings screen.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

Data Deleted

When an account is deleted, the following information is permanently removed:

  • User profile information.
  • Email address associated with the account.
  • Medication schedules and reminders.
  • Dose history and medication tracking records.
  • Caregiver relationships and invitations.
  • Push notification tokens.

Data Retention

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Once deleted, your account and associated data cannot be recovered.
